# Stone Miner Game
A 2D incremental game about mining stone made in Godot with GDScript.

You can play the game at: https://erzis.itch.io/stone-miner

# Core Loop
1. Mine Stone
2. Load it onto the lift
3. Sell the stone
4. Buy upgrades

# Mechanics
- Stone Collection: Each strike yields stone based on your minerâ€™s damage.
- Gold Earnings: Gold is calculated based on the amount of stone you bring to the surface.
- Experience & Leveling: Mining gives your miner experience. Leveling up unlocks new upgrades and increases your mining efficiency.
- Upgrades: Each upgrade has 5 tiers, providing incremental bonuses. Some upgrades unlock only when your miner reaches a certain level.
- Progression: The game is about optimizing mining, upgrading your miner, and maximizing gold output.
